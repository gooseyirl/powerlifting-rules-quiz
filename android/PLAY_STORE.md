## Long Description (4000 characters max)

```
DISCLAIMER: This is an unofficial, independent app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with the International Powerlifting Federation (IPF).

Master the IPF Technical Rules Book with this comprehensive quiz application!

Powerlifting Rules Quiz is the perfect study companion for powerlifters, coaches, referees, and anyone interested in International Powerlifting Federation competition rules. This app helps you learn and test your knowledge of the technical rules governing IPF powerlifting meets.

KEY FEATURES

📚 Comprehensive Question Bank
• Questions covering all major sections of the IPF Technical Rules Book
• Equipment specifications (bars, discs, platforms)
• Rules of performance (squat, bench press, deadlift)
• Judging procedures and referee signals
• Personal equipment regulations

🎯 Interactive Quiz Experience
• 10 randomly selected questions per quiz session
• Multiple-choice format for easy learning
• Visual progress tracking throughout the quiz
• Immediate feedback on your performance

📊 Detailed Results & Analytics
• Comprehensive score summary with percentage
• Pass/fail indication (70% passing threshold)
• Breakdown of correct and incorrect answers
• Performance tracking to monitor improvement

📖 In-Depth Answer Review
• Review every question and answer after completing the quiz
• See your answers compared to correct answers
• Detailed explanations for each question
• Exact rulebook references (section, rule number, page)

🎓 Learn as You Go
• Every question includes the specific rule reference
• Explanations help you understand the "why" behind each rule
• Perfect for studying before competitions or certification
• Great refresher for experienced lifters and officials

✨ Clean, Modern Design
• Material Design 3 interface
• IPF-themed color scheme
• Easy-to-read text and clear layouts
• Smooth navigation and intuitive controls

🔒 Privacy-First
• Completely offline - no internet required
• No data collection or tracking
• No permissions required

WHO IS THIS FOR?

• Powerlifters preparing for IPF competitions
• Coaches training athletes in IPF rules
• Aspiring and current IPF referees
• Meet directors and technical officials
• Anyone interested in learning powerlifting rules

BASED ON OFFICIAL RULES

All questions are based on the IPF Technical Rules Book (March 2026 version). The app is regularly updated to reflect the latest rule changes and clarifications.

PERFECT FOR EXAM PREPARATION

If you're preparing for IPF referee certification or simply want to ensure you understand the technical requirements for competition, this app provides an efficient and engaging way to study the rulebook.

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

The question bank is regularly expanded and updated to provide comprehensive coverage of all technical rules. Each question is carefully crafted to test real understanding, not just memorization.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Start a quiz with 10 random questions
2. Select your answers from multiple choices
3. Submit your quiz and see your results
4. Review answers to learn from mistakes
5. Retake the quiz to improve your score

NO TRACKING, NO HASSLE

Focus on learning without distractions. This app is built for education, not data collection. Everything works offline, so you can study anywhere, anytime.

Download now and become an expert in IPF technical rules!

```
